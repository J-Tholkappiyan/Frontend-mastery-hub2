ABOUT US

Our journey and philosophy

OUR STORY

Founded in 2010 by former national athlete Rajesh Kumar, Master Sports Academy began as a small training center with just two coaches and a single cricket pitch. Today, we've grown into one of the premier sports training facilities in the country, spanning over 10 acres with world-class facilities for multiple sports.

Our journey has been marked by numerous success stories - from training state-level champions to nurturing athletes who have represented India at international competitions.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

At Master Sports Academy, we believe in holistic athlete development. We don't just train players - we build character, discipline, and sportsmanship. Our "Education First" policy ensures our athletes excel academically while pursuing their sporting dreams.
